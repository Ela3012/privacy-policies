Privacy Policy

1. Information Collected

When you use our Discord bot, we collect and store certain information to provide its services. This includes:

  • **User IDs**: The bot may collect user IDs, server (guild) IDs, and messages to execute commands.
  • **Wattpad URLs**: When you use the bot to follow a story or profile, we store the story or profile URL to monitor updates.
  • **Activity Logs**: We may store logs to help identify and resolve technical issues or abuses.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Execute bot commands and provide update notifications.
  • Improve the bot's functionality and address any issues that arise.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties. However, we reserve the right to disclose information if required by law or to respond to legal requests.

4. Information Storage

Data is stored locally on the server hosting the bot. It is not transferred to third-party servers. The data is retained for as long as you use the bot. If you stop following a story or profile, the associated data will be deleted.

5. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect the information stored by the bot from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us through Discord.
  • Request access to the data we store about you.

7. Modifications

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted here, and you will be notified of any significant updates.

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us through Discord.
